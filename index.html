Dip-and-Recover Trading Bot

Two paper portfolios, same signals, different position sizing.
Strategy: buy any S&P 500 stock down 5%+ vs. previous close, sell once a position recovers 7%+ from its average entry price. Both portfolios use the same buy/sell signals and started with $500,000 — Portfolio 2 simply bets 25% more per trade ($12,500 vs. $10,000). Runs automatically every 15 minutes during market hours via GitHub Actions.
This is a paper-trading demo, not financial advice, and not a proven profitable strategy.
