Dip-and-Recover Trading Bot

Two paper portfolios: Portfolio 1 runs fixed rules, Portfolio 2 tunes its own strategy as it trades.
Both portfolios started with $500,000 and buy S&P 500 stocks that drop vs. the previous close. Portfolio 1 runs fixed rules: buy on a 5%+ drop, sell once a position recovers 7%+. Portfolio 2 runs the same dip-and-recover idea but adaptively: it tunes its own buy-dip threshold, sell target, and position size run over run based on how its own trades go, aiming to land each completed trade's gain in a 10-15% band — a bounded, rule-based auto-tuner, not machine learning; every parameter has a hard floor/ceiling it can never cross. Portfolio 2 can also size a single trade up to 2x its normal ceiling on unusually deep, high-conviction dips. Both portfolios share the same risk controls: a hard stop-loss, a max hold time, a cap on concurrent positions and per-sector exposure, and a market-regime filter that pauses new buys on a broad S&P 500 selloff day. Runs automatically every 5 minutes during market hours via GitHub Actions.
This is a paper-trading demo, not financial advice, and not a proven profitable strategy.
